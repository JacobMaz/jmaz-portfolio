About Me

Hello, I'm Jacob Mazanowski. I grew up in Indiana for the first 22 years of my life and love that I call it home. Looking for a little adventure, I decided to move to Hawaii where I spent three years of my life exploring the island of Oahu. After I moved back home to Indiana, I met my amazing wife and had a son. He's a little crazy but he get that from me. My free time is usally spent going to the park with my family or playing my guitar. To relax, I love playing video games or watching sports. Overall, I am a happy guy who loves what I do and am blessed with the life I have.

In my mid twenties, I became an apprentice brewer. I loved it! I loved the detail and care that went into every batch of beer that we created and loved learning everything I could about the process. After I left the brewing industry and accepted a job in landscaping, I felt myself longing for a creative outlet and seeking to create something useful. That's when a friend suggested I look into web development and the Eleven Fifty Academy. Learning all things I have about Web development, coding, and tech has been more satisfying than I could have ever hoped for. I have really enjoyed getting involed in this industry and cannot wait to dive in deeper!

Language: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, TypeScript
